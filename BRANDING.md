# Vespera Brand

Visual identity for Vespera, the rental payments platform on Stellar.

## Wordmark

Vespera, set in a serif display face. Latin for "evening star". Always one word.

## Colors

| token | hex | use |
|---|---|---|
| Dusk Pink | `#f4a8c1` | primary accent |
| Twilight Violet | `#7a3a7e` | secondary surface |
| Cream | `#fff5e1` | text on dark, star highlights |
| Night Purple | `#2d1840` | dark surfaces, dusk |
| Deep Indigo | `#1a0c2a` | absolute background |

## Logo files

- `assets/vespera-logo.svg` — square mark with crescent and evening star
- `assets/vespera-banner.svg` — wide banner with wordmark and twilight sky

## Voice

Calm, human, respectful. We talk about families, homes and rent, not "users" and "transactions". Plain language over jargon.

## Don't

- do not use the wordmark in all caps
- do not use neon colours adjacent to the brand
- do not depict the crescent inside the star or vice versa
